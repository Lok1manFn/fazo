Bouquet care recommendations

1 Rule

Fill a vase with cold water and add ⅓ of a sachet of CRYZAL, which we include with each bouquet, to it. Repeat the procedure every 2-3 days.

2 Rule

Cut the stems of the flowers with a sharp tool, at an acute angle. Repeat the procedure when changing the water every 2-3 days.

3 Rule

Avoid direct sunlight and drafts. Keep away from heaters and fruit.
